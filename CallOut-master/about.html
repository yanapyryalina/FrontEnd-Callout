Giving women the power to standu up for themselves

So, we've all been in these uncomfortable situations in the workplace. All the "You should smile more!", "Ooo, she's a piece of work", "I think what she's *actually* trying to sayâ€¦.", "Is it that time of the month?" - unfortunately, these situations are still extremely relatable to women in the workplace. At times like this, we cannot always stand up for ourselves. What if we say too much? What if we come off as overeating? What will happen to our jobs after that? Our mission is to allow women to practice standing up for themselves. We hope that by practicing in simulated situations on CallOut, you will be able to say "I can do this, I've done this a hundred times by now", and choose your own way to confidently say what you think and ask for what you want.

On another note, we would like to make a disclaimer. We do NOT wish to dehumanize and stereotype men as sexist. Doing so would be a great disservice those who truly respect, support, and protect women. We are incredibly grateful for those who have supported us throughout this journey, and look forward to building services to also help men practice standing up for themselves.
